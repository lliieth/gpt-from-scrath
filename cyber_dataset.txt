Cybersecurity protects systems and networks from digital attacks.

Malware is malicious software designed to damage systems.

Phishing attacks trick users into revealing sensitive information.

Ransomware encrypts files and demands payment.

A vulnerability is a weakness that attackers exploit.

Encryption protects sensitive data from unauthorized access.

Firewalls monitor and control network traffic.

Authentication verifies a user's identity.

Intrusion detection systems monitor networks for attacks.

Security analysts investigate cyber threats.
